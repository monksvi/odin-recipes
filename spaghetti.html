Classic Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Ground pork
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Canned tomato sauce
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Fresh parsley
  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the Meatballs:
    • In a large bowl, mix together ground beef, ground pork, breadcrumbs, eggs, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
    • Form the mixture into meatballs, about 1.5 inches in diameter, and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake the Meatballs: Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are cooked through and browned.
  4. Prepare the Sauce:
    • While the meatballs are baking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    • Add chopped onions and cook until they are soft and translucent.
    • Add minced garlic and cook for an additional 1 minute.
    • Stir in the canned tomato sauce and diced tomatoes. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
    • Add fresh basil and parsley to the sauce. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Cook the Spaghetti: Cook spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  6. Add Meatballs to the Sauce: Add the baked meatballs to the simmering sauce and let them cook for an additional 10 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  7. Combine with Spaghetti: Toss the cooked spaghetti with the meatball and tomato sauce mixture, or serve the meatballs and sauce over the spaghetti.
  8. Serve: Garnish with extra Parmesan cheese and fresh parsley if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!
