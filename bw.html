Beef Wellington

Ingredients

  • Beef tenderloin (center-cut, about 2 pounds)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Mushrooms (finely chopped, about 12 ounces)
  • Shallots (finely chopped, 2 tablespoons)
  • Garlic (minced, 2 cloves)
  • Fresh thyme (1 tablespoon)
  • Puff pastry (about 1 pound, thawed if frozen)
  • Prosciutto (slices, 6-8 pieces)
  • Dijon mustard (2 tablespoons)
  • Egg yolk (for egg wash, 1 yolk)
  • Flour (for dusting, as needed)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Beef:
    • Season the beef tenderloin generously with salt and black pepper.
    • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over high heat.
    • Sear the beef on all sides until browned, about 2-3 minutes per side.
    • Transfer the beef to a plate and let it cool. Brush the beef with Dijon mustard.
  2. Prepare the Mushroom Duxelles:
    • In the same skillet, melt butter over medium heat.
    • Add finely chopped mushrooms, shallots, and minced garlic.
    • Cook until the mushrooms release their moisture and become golden brown, about 10 minutes.
    • Add fresh thyme and season with salt and black pepper. Let cool.
  3. Assemble the Wellington:
    • Lay out the prosciutto slices on a sheet of plastic wrap, slightly overlapping.
    • Spread the mushroom duxelles evenly over the prosciutto.
    • Place the beef tenderloin on top of the duxelles and roll it up tightly in the prosciutto using the plastic wrap. Chill in the refrigerator for 15 minutes.
    • Roll out the puff pastry on a lightly floured surface to a rectangle large enough to wrap around the beef.
    • Remove the beef from the plastic wrap and place it in the center of the puff pastry.
    • Fold the pastry over the beef and seal the edges. Brush the pastry with beaten egg yolk.

    • Place the wrapped beef on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    • Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown and the internal temperature of the beef reaches 130째F (54째C) for medium-rare.

  5. Serve: Slice the Beef Wellington and serve with your favorite sides. Enjoy your delicious and elegant meal!
