Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta or Guanciale
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Prepare the Meat:
    • While the pasta is cooking, cut the pancetta or guanciale into small cubes.
    • Heat a bit of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    • Add the pancetta or guanciale to the skillet and cook until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Sauce:
    • In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and grated Pecorino Romano cheese.
    • Add freshly ground black pepper to the egg mixture. Set aside.
  4. Combine Ingredients:
    • Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta or guanciale. Toss to combine and heat through.
    • Remove the skillet from the heat. Quickly pour in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing the pasta continuously to create a creamy sauce.
    • Add a bit of the reserved pasta water to achieve the desired consistency of the sauce.
  5. Serve: Serve the Carbonara immediately with extra Parmesan cheese and freshly ground black pepper if desired.
